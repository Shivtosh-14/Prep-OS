Writing & Language (12 Questions)

16. Choose the correct option:

The student, along with her classmates, [is/are] going to the museum.

17. Choose the correct punctuation:

The research findings, [which/that] were published last month, surprised many scientists.

18. Choose the correct verb form:

Neither the students nor the teacher [was/were] prepared for the surprise quiz.

19. Choose the most concise option:

The reason [why/that] the experiment failed was due to contaminated samples.

20. Choose the correct pronoun:

Each of the students must submit [their/his or her] assignment by Friday.

21. Choose the correct modifier:

[Walking quickly, the bus] was caught by the student.

22. Choose the correct punctuation:

The meeting will be held on Tuesday, March 15 [;/,] at 3:00 PM.

23. Choose the correct parallel structure:

The student enjoys reading, writing, and [to study/studying] mathematics.

24. Choose the most effective word:

The results were [very/extremely] significant for the research team.

25. Choose the correct sentence structure:

[Having finished the project, the presentation was ready.]

26. Choose the correct verb tense:

By the time the semester ends, the students [will have completed/will complete] all their assignments.

27. Choose the correct word order:

The professor, [who/whom] the students greatly admired, retired last year.

🔢 Math Section (27 Questions)

Algebra (9 Questions)

28. If 3x + 7 = 22, what is the value of x?

29. If f(x) = 2x² - 3x + 1, what is f(2)?

30. If 2x + 3y = 12 and x - y = 1, what is the value of x?

31. What is the solution to the inequality 2x - 5 > 7?

32. If log₂(x) = 4, what is the value of x?

33. What is the domain of f(x) = √(x - 3)?

34. If a car rental company charges a flat fee of $50 plus $0.25 per mile driven, and a customer spends $95 total, how many miles did they drive?

35. What is the vertex of the parabola y = x² - 4x + 3?

36. What is the sum of the solutions to x² - 5x + 6 = 0?

Geometry (9 Questions)

37. A circle has a radius of 5 cm. What is its area in square centimeters?

38. In a right triangle, if one angle is 30° and the hypotenuse is 10, what is the length of the side opposite the 30° angle?

39. What is the distance between points (2, 3) and (5, 7)?

40. A rectangular garden has a length that is 3 times its width. If the perimeter is 48 feet, what is the area of the garden?

41. What is the volume of a cylinder with radius 3 and height 8?

42. In triangle ABC, if angle A = 60°, angle B = 45°, and side a = 6, what is the length of side b?

43. What is the equation of a circle with center (3, -2) and radius 4?

44. What is the surface area of a cube with side length 4?

45. If two parallel lines are cut by a transversal, and one of the corresponding angles measures 70°, what is the measure of the other corresponding angle?

Statistics & Probability (9 Questions)

46. What is the probability of rolling a 6 on a fair six-sided die?

47. If the mean of five numbers is 20, and four of the numbers are 15, 18, 22, and 25, what is the fifth number?

48. What is the median of the data set: 3, 7, 9, 12, 15, 18, 21?

49. If two events are independent and P(A) = 0.3 and P(B) = 0.4, what is P(A and B)?

50. What is the range of the data set: 5, 8, 12, 15, 18, 22, 25?

51. If a bag contains 3 red marbles, 4 blue marbles, and 5 green marbles, what is the probability of drawing a red marble?

52. What is the standard deviation of the data set: 2, 4, 6, 8, 10?

53. If the mode of a data set is 15 and the mean is 18, which of the following could be the median?

54. What is the probability of getting exactly 2 heads when flipping a coin 3 times?
